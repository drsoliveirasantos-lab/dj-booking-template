Promoter FAQ

What events fit best?

Clubs, private parties, festivals, Latin/Brazilian nights and cultural events that need high-energy rhythm and dancefloor movement.

How to book?

Send the city, event date, type of event, approximate guests and budget through WhatsApp for a fast availability check.

Does he travel?

The site is positioned for bookings across Europe. Travel details can be confirmed during the booking request.

What materials are available?

Bio, photos, social links and booking information. Real videos and final press photos can be added when provided by the artist.
